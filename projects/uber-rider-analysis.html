Uber Rider Analysis

Ride-hailing platforms generate large volumes of trip data, but without structured analysis it's difficult to understand demand patterns, fare behavior, and rider trends well enough to support business decisions.

View on GitHub

1Problem Statement

Ride-hailing platforms generate large volumes of trip data, but without structured analysis it's difficult to understand demand patterns, fare behavior, and rider trends well enough to support business decisions.

2Approach & Solution

Performed exploratory data analysis (EDA) on 1000+ Uber ride records — cleaning the raw trip data, engineering features around time, location and fare, and building visualizations to uncover demand patterns and fare/distance relationships. The analysis also covers KPI-style summaries (ride volume, revenue trends, customer behavior) so the notebook doubles as both an analytical study and a lightweight reporting dashboard.
